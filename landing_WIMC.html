About

The story
behind WIMC

As a father, there is one feeling I know well: that quiet unease when you don't know where your child is. Not panic — just a low hum of worry that sits in the back of your mind until you get a message, a call, or they walk through the door.

I wanted something simple. Not an app that turns into surveillance, not a tool that makes my daughter feel watched every minute — but a discreet way to know she's safe, without being intrusive. Something that respects her independence while giving me peace of mind.

WIMC was born from that need. Built with real GPS tracking, background location updates, and smart safe zones — it's designed to be a safety net, not a leash.

This project is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, built as part of the Full-Stack & AI Development program in Bordeaux.
